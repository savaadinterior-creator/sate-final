About

Interior designer in Porto for characterful homes

I am Alina Savankova, an architect and interior designer based in Porto with over 8 years of experience. I work with apartments and houses across Portugal, creating spaces that feel calm, personal and carefully considered.

My approach is quiet and practical: I look for thoughtful layouts, balanced materials, comfortable proportions and details that support everyday life without making the interior feel overdesigned.

For me, interior design is not about decoration first. It is about the environment a person lives in — how morning light enters the room, how materials feel to the touch, how the layout gives a sense of calm, privacy and ease.

Process

How we work

01 Discovery call

We discuss your property, lifestyle, goals, timing and the type of support you need.

02 Brief & project diagnosis

I analyse the space, your needs, references, budget logic and possible design direction.

03 Layout and concept

We define the planning solution, zoning, atmosphere, colour direction and key interior decisions.

04 Design development

The concept is developed into visual materials, specifications and technical documentation.

05 Sourcing & implementation support

If needed, I help with furniture, materials, suppliers and author's supervision during the implementation stage.
